Event Details

Join us for the annual Harvest Festival on October 14th from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM at the Community Town Square. Enjoy fresh seasonal produce, live entertainment, and family-friendly activities celebrating the fall season.

Activities

  • Pumpkin Decorating: Kids and adults can decorate their own pumpkins.
  • Apple Tasting: Sample fresh apples from local orchards.
  • Live Music: Local musicians performing throughout the day.
  • Farmers Market: Purchase fresh produce and handmade goods.

Special Guests

Sarah Green
Local farmer sharing tips on sustainable agriculture.
Chef Daniel Lopez
Guest chef hosting a fall cooking demonstration.

Local band providing live music for the festival.
